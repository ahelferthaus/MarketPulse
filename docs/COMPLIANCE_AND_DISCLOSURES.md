# Westwood MarketPulse — Compliance & Disclosures

## Regulatory Disclosures

### Not Investment Advice
**Westwood MarketPulse is an educational market-sentiment tool. It is NOT investment advice, a recommendation to buy or sell any security, or a trading signal.**

- MarketPulse scores are derived from market data and text analysis for informational purposes only.
- Past performance of any index or regime is not indicative of future results.
- Users should not make investment decisions based solely on MarketPulse readings.
- Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

### Data Disclaimers

**Delayed Data**: The public version of MarketPulse uses delayed data (typically 15-20 minutes for prices, up to 24 hours for some economic series). The timestamp of each score indicates the freshness of the underlying data.

**Derived Scores Only**: The public website displays only derived sentiment scores and regime labels. No raw market data, security prices, or proprietary data is exposed through the public API or embed widgets.

**Source Transparency**: Every score includes a data quality report showing which sources were used, which components were substituted, and the overall confidence level. Users should review this information before interpreting any score.

**Proxy Data**: When primary data sources are unavailable, MarketPulse uses proxy data (e.g., using ETF data when index data is unavailable). These substitutions are clearly labeled and reduce the confidence score accordingly.

### Professional Data Licensing

**Bloomberg Data**: Bloomberg data is licensed for internal Westwood use only. No Bloomberg data, identifiers, or raw feeds are distributed through the public MarketPulse website. The daily export workflow generates sanitized JSON files containing only derived scores and labels.

**Third-Party APIs**: MarketPulse may use data from Financial Modeling Prep, CBOE, FRED, NewsAPI, and other third-party services. All such use complies with the respective terms of service.

**News Content**: News headlines and summaries displayed in MarketPulse are sourced from publicly available RSS feeds and APIs. Full article content is not reproduced. Links direct users to original sources.

### No Guarantees

Westwood makes no representations or warranties of any kind, express or implied, about:
- The accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of any score or data
- The suitability of MarketPulse for any particular purpose
- The availability or uptime of the service
- The security of data transmitted through the service

### Forward-Looking Statements

Any forward-looking analysis generated by MarketPulse (including "what to watch" and regime-based return expectations) is based on historical patterns and statistical analysis. These are not predictions and should not be relied upon as such. Market conditions can change rapidly and unpredictably.

### Embedded Widget Terms

Websites embedding MarketPulse widgets must:
- Maintain the "Powered by Westwood MarketPulse" attribution
- Not modify the data or scores displayed
- Not present the widget as their own proprietary analysis
- Link to the full methodology page

### Privacy

MarketPulse does not collect personal information from public users. Embed widgets do not use cookies or tracking pixels. API usage is logged for rate limiting and service quality purposes only.
